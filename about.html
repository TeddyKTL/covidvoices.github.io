The COVID pandemic is real and a serious global crisis.

Please practice physical distancing.
Please restrain from any non-essential travel.
Please keep people in mind who are at risk.

CovidVoices is a project with journalistic and academic focus. It is executed by professionals who are constantly consulting with experts in the field and on the crisis.

During the journey, Teddy himself is self-isolating by
1) Always wearing a mask and gloves in places where other people are present
2) Conducting interviews and conversations by maintaining at least 20 feet of distance
3) Living in a vehicle
4) Limiting shopping for essentials to a maximum of every two weeks.
